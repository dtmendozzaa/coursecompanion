Course Overview

Module 1: More Than a Game

Understand what golf really means to your child — how their identity, emotions, and confidence are often tied to performance.

Module 2: Pressure & Perception

Explore how internal and external pressure affect your child’s mindset — and how to help relieve it instead of amplify it.

Module 3: What Not to Say

Learn the common phrases and reactions that can unintentionally hurt your child’s confidence or relationship with golf.

Module 4: Building Trust

Discover strategies to strengthen your bond so your child feels supported, not judged — even after tough rounds.

Module 5: Scripts & Real-Life Tools

Get access to real conversations, examples, and templates you can start using today to support your athlete better.
