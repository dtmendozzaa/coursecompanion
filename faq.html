Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course just for golf parents?

Nope! While the course is built around junior golf, the emotional dynamics covered apply to any competitive sport where performance affects self-worth and family relationships.

How long is the course?

The course is broken into short, digestible modules—perfect for busy parents. You can go at your own pace, and revisit lessons any time.

Will this teach me how to coach my child?

This isn’t about swing tips or sports mechanics. It’s about emotional coaching—how to be someone your athlete can trust and lean on, especially when things aren’t going well.

My child doesn’t talk much after rounds. Will this still help?

Absolutely. You’ll learn how to read their mood, what not to say, and how to support them without shutting them down—even if they don’t open up right away.

What if I’m not a “sports parent”?

You don’t need to be. This course is about connection and understanding—something any parent can benefit from, regardless of their own athletic background.
